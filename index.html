After receiving your job order, we begin by understanding your organization’s needs or requirements for IT engineers or ICT workers. You’ll have an option of a work setup for the candidate. You can choose from Offshore, Remote, and Onshore deployment.

match

We connect with Oh! Jobs, to source the best candidates that best fit your requirements. We thoroughly review resumes and credentials to sort qualified candidates ready for you to interview.

assessment

They undergo a special assessment that evaluates their technical skills and knowledge. Our reference checks ensure we are dealing with the right candidate. On final selection, we provide you with a shortlist and comprehensive feedback on each candidate’s strengths, weaknesses, and suitability.
recruit
We extend the job offer to the selected IT engineers and handle salary negotiations, benefits, and contracts. We also verify the contract to comply with the prevailing laws in the Philippines and the host country.
tend
We manage and provide pre-departure orientation and handle permit, document, and deployment processes, including medical, travel, and visa processing coordination. We utilize Hi! Molly, to establish connections between employers and their employees,  regardless of whether they work remotely or offshore.
